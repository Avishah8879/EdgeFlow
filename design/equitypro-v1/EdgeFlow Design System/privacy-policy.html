1 · Who we are

EquityPro Capital Markets Pvt. Ltd. ("EquityPro", "we") is a SEBI-registered Research Analyst (INH000012345) operating an investment-research platform for the Indian capital markets. This policy applies to equitypro.in, our mobile applications, and the public API at api.equitypro.in.

We are the data fiduciary for personal data you provide while signing up, browsing, configuring screens and backtests, and connecting brokers. The Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("DPDP") governs this relationship.

2 · Data we collect

2.1 Account data

Email, hashed password, full name, mobile number, country, time-zone, profile photo (optional), and PAN-derived KYC reference if you connect a broker.

2.2 Usage data

Pages viewed, screens saved, backtests run (configuration + result hashes), clicks, search queries inside our app, IP address, and a stable device fingerprint used to prevent token theft.

2.3 Market activity

If you opt in to broker sync: holdings, transaction history, F&O positions, and order placements made via EquityPro. We do not read funds movements or bank statements.

3 · How we use it

  • Operate the service — render dashboards, execute screens, backtest strategies, deliver alerts.
  • Improve the platform — aggregate, anonymized analytics on which screens work and which fail.
  • Communicate — transactional email (token expiry, billing), and opt-in newsletters you can unsubscribe from in one click.
  • Comply — SEBI/RBI/CERT-In reporting where required, fraud prevention, tax compliance.

4 · Sharing & processors

We never sell your data. We share it only with:

  • Cloud infrastructure — AWS Mumbai (ap-south-1) and Cloudflare for edge.
  • Email & payments — Postmark, Stripe India, Razorpay.
  • Brokers — only when you explicitly initiate an action (placing an order, fetching holdings).
  • Regulators — when compelled by valid legal process under Indian law.

5 · Broker tokens

Plain English first. When you connect Fyers, Zerodha or another broker, the broker — not us — authenticates you. We receive a short-lived access token, never your password.

Tokens are encrypted with per-tenant keys (AES-256-GCM) and stored in a separate secrets vault. Decryption keys are held in AWS KMS with audit-logged access. Disconnecting at oauth-setup revokes the token at the broker and deletes our copy within 60 seconds.

6 · Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. After deletion: 30 days soft-delete window (account recoverable) → full purge within 90 days, except where law requires longer (financial-records: 8 years under SEBI rules).

7 · Your rights under DPDP

  • Access — request a machine-readable copy from profile → "Export data".
  • Correction — edit profile fields anytime; structured corrections via support.
  • Erasure — delete your account from settings. Some logs persist for fraud-prevention.
  • Portability — JSON + CSV bundle includes screens, backtests, watchlists, notes.
  • Grievance redressal — escalate to our Grievance Officer (see §10) within 30 days.

8 · Cookies

We use first-party cookies for session management (ep_session), CSRF protection (ep_csrf), and theme preference (ep_theme). We use a single privacy-focused analytics cookie (ep_id) that does not cross domains.

9 · Security

TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest, weekly third-party penetration tests, SOC-2 Type II audited annually. Password hashing uses Argon2id with per-user salt. We are CERT-In empanelled and report incidents within statutory windows.
