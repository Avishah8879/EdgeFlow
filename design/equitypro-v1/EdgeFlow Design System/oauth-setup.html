How OAuth works on EquityPro

From "Connect" to live trading in four steps

1

Click Connect

You'll be redirected to your broker's login page on their domain — never on ours.

2

Approve scopes

Grant read-only or trade permissions. We never see your password.

3

Token exchange

Broker redirects back. We exchange the code for an access token, encrypted at rest.

4

Trade & sync

Holdings appear in Portfolio; Place-Order buttons go live across all screens.
