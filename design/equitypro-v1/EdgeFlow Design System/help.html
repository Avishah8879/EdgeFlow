Frequently asked

How do I connect my Fyers account?

Go to Account → Broker integration, click Connect Fyers, and you'll be redirected to Fyers' OAuth screen. Approve the read-only scopes and you'll come back to EquityPro automatically. Tokens are stored encrypted (AES-256) and refresh every 24 hours.

If your token expired, click the Re-authorize banner and re-do the OAuth flow — takes 15 seconds.

Why is my watchlist quote 15 minutes delayed?

Free plans show end-of-day data. Pro and Quant plans include NSE realtime feed (sub-second tick) for all listed equities & F&O. Upgrade from Account → Plan.

Can I export backtest results to PDF or Excel?

Yes. From any backtest report, click the Export button. PDF includes the full curve, drawdown chart, monthly heatmap, and trade list. Excel exports the trade ledger, daily returns, and rolling Sharpe. Pro plan and above.

How is my data secured?

All credentials are encrypted at rest (AES-256-GCM) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We never store your broker password — only OAuth tokens with read-only scopes. SOC 2 Type II audited annually. Data residency: AWS Mumbai (ap-south-1).

Do you have a refund policy?

Yes — 14-day no-questions-asked refund on all annual plans. Monthly plans can be cancelled any time with no penalty. Refunds processed within 5 business days to the original payment method.

How many devices can I use?

Pro plan: 3 simultaneous sessions. Quant plan: 5. Web, iOS, and Android each count as one session. You can manage active sessions and revoke them from Account → Security.
