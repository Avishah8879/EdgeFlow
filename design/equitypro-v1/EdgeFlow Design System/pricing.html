FAQ

Common questions.

Is my market data really live?

On Pro and Quant, yes — we receive a SEBI-licensed real-time NSE feed. The Starter tier shows the same feed at a 15-minute delay, which is fine for end-of-day investing.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your billing page; you keep access until the period ends. All your saved screens, backtests, and notes export as CSV/JSON before deactivation.

Do you give investment advice?

No. EquityPro is a research terminal. We are SEBI-registered as a research analyst (INH000xxxxxx) — we publish data and tools, never recommendations on individual securities for individual users.

How accurate is the QGA backtester?

The engine tests on point-in-time data with realistic slippage and survivorship-bias correction. All results are out-of-sample on a 20 % holdout. We publish full methodology in the docs.

Can I use the Quant API for production trading?

Yes — the Quant tier ships with a stable REST + WebSocket API, 60 req/sec, with HMAC-signed webhooks. Trading bots are your own to build; we focus on research and signals.

GST and invoicing?

All listed prices exclude 18 % GST. Invoices are generated automatically with your GSTIN; reach out for proforma invoices or annual prepay options.
